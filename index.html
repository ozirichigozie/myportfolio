Executive Summary
In today's world, it is clearly evident that most inventions and innovations in virtually every industry are technology-driven. Successful entrepreneurship now demands efficient use of digital skills if potential must be maximised and/or globalization achieved. This has posed a great need to raise the level of digital awareness among students and entrepreneurs in order to enhance their competence and competitiveness in the global market. I am Chigozie Cosmos Oziri, a growing tech entrepreneur focusing on providing I.T solutions and education to individuals, businesses and academic institutions.

Vision
Information technology is undoubtedly both the present and future of the world. My vision is to prepare the African youth for inevitable success in entrepreneurship and career pursuit in their chosen industry through I.T education, and also to provide I.T solutions to meet the needs of business owners across the globe.

Mission
I am looking forward to:

  • working with business entrepreneurs in order to equip them with the right tools to grow their businesses and maximise potential.
  • contributing my part to preparing the African student for the future of the world through digital awareness and skill acquisition.
  • discovering tech talents among students.
